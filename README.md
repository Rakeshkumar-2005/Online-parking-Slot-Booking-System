Title of the Project - 
Parking Slot Booking System

1. Problem Statement --
In urban areas, finding an available parking space is a major challenge. Drivers often spend a
significant amount of time searching for parking, leading to traffic congestion, fuel wastage,
and frustration. Traditional parking systems do not provide real-time information about slot
availability, making the parking process inefficient.
The proposed Parking Slot Booking System aims to provide a digital platform where users
can search, reserve, and pay for parking slots in advance, ensuring a smooth and convenient
parking experience.

3. Description of the Problem--
With the rapid growth of vehicles in cities, parking management has become increasingly
difficult. Many parking facilities operate manually, causing issues such as:
 Lack of real-time parking information.
 Time-consuming search for vacant slots.
 Traffic congestion near parking areas.
 Inefficient utilization of parking spaces.
 Difficulty in managing reservations and payments.
The system will solve these problems by providing an online booking platform with real-time
slot availability and digital payment facilities.

3. Objectives and Scope of the Project
Objectives-
 To provide a platform for searching available parking slots.
 To allow users to reserve parking spaces in advance.
 To enable secure online payment for parking reservations.
 To display real-time parking slot availability.
 To provide booking confirmation through QR code or PIN.
 To help parking owners manage slots and reservations efficiently

Scope-
The project can be used in:
 Shopping malls
 Airports
 Railway stations
 Hospitals
 Colleges and universities
 Smart city parking systems
The system benefits both vehicle owners and parking facility operators by improving
efficiency and reducing congestion.
